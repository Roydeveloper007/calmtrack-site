Built for Neurodiversity

CalmTrack is designed for Autism, ADHD, Sensory Processing Disorder, and other neurodevelopmental differences. Learn more about these conditions from trusted resources:

Autism ADHD Sensory Processing Tourette Syndrome Anxiety Other Conditions Asperger Profile Auditory Processing

Impact & Prevalence

  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) – affecting ~1 in 36 children
  • ADHD – impacts approximately 8.7% of children and 4.4% of adults globally
  • Sensory Processing Disorder (SPD) – commonly co-occurs with autism
  • Tourette Syndrome – affects 1 in 162 children in the U.S.

Care Needs

  • Ongoing monitoring to identify what helps and hinders
  • Data for specialists to make informed care decisions
  • Communication tools for school accommodations
  • Emotional regulation tracking for personal growth

Data Privacy & Protection

Your Data, Your Peace of Mind

  • Data is always encrypted and protected on your device — your family's information stays safe and private, always.
  • Automatic protection — CalmTrack quietly safeguards your information while you focus on what matters.
  • You stay in control — data is never shared, sold, or sent to anyone unless you choose to do so.
  • Peace of mind by design — every feature and update is built with your trust and privacy at the center.

Our Privacy Commitment

  • Local-First Storage: Your data is stored securely on your own device by default.
  • Encryption at Rest: When CalmTrack stores information, it is encrypted on your device.
  • No Automatic Data Sharing: CalmTrack does not share your personal data with any third party.
  • Explicit User Actions: Only when you choose to generate a report is your data processed for that specific action.
  • No Tracking or Analytics: We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics that collect personal information.
  • Transparent Controls: You can delete your data at any time from within the app.

We designed CalmTrack to put you in control. Our privacy approach is simple: your data, your device, your rules.
