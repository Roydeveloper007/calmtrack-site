Designed for Neurodiversity

CalmTrack is thoughtfully designed for Autism Spectrum Disorder, ADHD, Sensory Processing Disorder, and other neurodevelopmental differences. Explore these conditions through trusted, evidence-based resources:

Autism Spectrum ADHD Sensory Processing Tourette Syndrome Anxiety Disorders PTSD Other Conditions

Prevalence & Impact

  • Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) – affects approximately 1 in 36 children
  • ADHD – impacts 8.7% of children and 4.4% of adults globally
  • Sensory Processing Disorder – commonly co-occurs with autism and ADHD
  • Anxiety Disorders – affects up to 30% of neurodivergent individuals
  • Co-occurring conditions – many individuals have multiple diagnoses

Care Needs

  • Continuous monitoring to identify effective strategies and triggers
  • Data-driven insights for specialists to make informed decisions
  • School communication tools for educational accommodations
  • Emotional regulation tracking for personal development
  • Family coordination across multiple caregivers and settings

Uncompromising Privacy & Security

Your Data, Your Complete Control

  • Military-grade encryption protects your family's sensitive information on your device at all times
  • Zero-knowledge architecture ensures even we cannot access your personal data
  • Granular sharing controls let you decide exactly what information gets shared and with whom
  • Privacy by design means every feature is built with your family's trust and security as the foundation

Our Privacy Commitment

  • Local-First Storage: Your behavioral data is stored securely on your own device by default
  • End-to-End Encryption: All data is encrypted both at rest and in transit
  • No Data Mining: We never analyze, sell, or monetize your personal information
  • Explicit Consent: Data sharing only occurs when you explicitly choose to generate and send reports
  • GDPR & HIPAA Compliant: Meeting the highest international privacy standards
  • Transparent Deletion: Permanently delete your data at any time with a single tap

We designed CalmTrack with absolute privacy in mind. Your sensitive family data, your trusted device, your complete control.
