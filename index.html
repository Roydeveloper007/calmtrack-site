Welcome to CalmTrack

A Digital Diary and Insight Tool for Neurodiverse Conditions

Vision

To empower individuals and caregivers with a simple yet powerful tool to track emotional and behavioral health, identify triggers, and facilitate well-informed care and medical decisions.

Mission

We aim to streamline behavioral and emotional tracking through intuitive, customizable logging and analysis tools, enabling parents, caregivers, and healthcare professionals to provide personalized care to neurodiverse individuals.

Data Privacy & Protection

  • Local-First Storage: Your data is stored securely on your own device by default. This means your information remains private and under your control unless you choose to back it up or share it.
  • Encryption at Rest: When CalmTrack stores information, it is encrypted on your device. This helps protect your data from unauthorized access.
  • No Automatic Data Sharing: CalmTrack does not share your personal data or behavioral records with any third party or server unless you explicitly choose to export, email, or back up your data.
  • Explicit User Actions: Only when you choose to generate a report or email a summary, is your data processed for that specific action, and never transmitted elsewhere without your consent.
  • No Tracking or Analytics: We do not use cookies, trackers, or analytics scripts that collect behavioral or personal information.
  • Transparent Controls: You can delete your data at any time from within the app. No hidden backups are made without your knowledge.

We designed CalmTrack to put you in control. Our privacy approach is simple: your data, your device, your rules.
