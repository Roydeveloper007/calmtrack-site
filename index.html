Welcome to CalmTrack

A Digital Diary and Insight Tool for Neurodiverse Conditions

Vision

To empower individuals and caregivers with a simple yet powerful tool to track emotional and behavioral health, identify triggers, and facilitate well-informed care and medical decisions.

Mission

We aim to streamline behavioral and emotional tracking through intuitive, customizable logging and analysis tools, enabling parents, caregivers, and healthcare professionals to provide personalized care to neurodiverse individuals.
