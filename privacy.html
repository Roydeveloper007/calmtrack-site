Privacy Policy

At CalmTrack, we value your privacy. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. What We Collect

  • Email address (for login and verification)
  • Basic profile info (name, age, profession)
  • Behavioral tracking logs (stored securely)

2. How We Use It

Data is used solely for in-app features like behavior trend analysis, reminders, and account access. We never sell your data.

3. Security

All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use AWS and industry-standard practices to ensure data security.

4. Contact

If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@calmtrack.app.
